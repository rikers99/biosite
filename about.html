Birth

Steve was born on 3 Feb 1967 in Altamonte Springs, Florida to Robert and Sandra Culmer and he has 1 brother, David.

His mom remarried 2 more times and his dad remarried 1 more time and he ended up having a half brother who is married and living in Los Angeles, California, and he has 4 stepbrothers who are living in Florida.

High School

Steve graduated from Land O' Lakes Senior High School in Land O' Lakes, Florida on May, 1985.

Military History

Steve joined the US Air Force right after graduating high school on 15 dec 1985 and served till Jul, 1995.

  • Basic Training 1985
  • Technical School for Munitions Systems Specialist 1986
  • 1st Assignment at Misawa AB, Japan 1986 to 1988
  • Temporary duty assignment to Kunsan, Korea in jul 1987
  • 2nd Assignment to Mtn. Home Air Force Base, Idaho from 1988 to 1995
  • Temporary Duty to AFCOMAC in Herlong, California in 1989
  • Temporary Duty to Al Karj, Saudi Arabia from 1990 to 1991 for the 1st Persian Gulf War(Operation Desert Shield, Storm and Calm).

Marriage

Steve was married on 18 apr 2003 to 21 aug 2010 when his wife passed from cancer.
